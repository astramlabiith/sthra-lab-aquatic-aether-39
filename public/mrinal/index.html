Why Mrinal? The Urgent Need

The global aquaculture industry, a vital source of protein, is increasingly threatened by widespread disease, environmental stress, and significant economic losses. Current monitoring methods are inherently flawed – they are often invasive, reactive, and incapable of providing the real-time, comprehensive data needed for effective management. This leads to costly interventions, reduced yields, and unsustainable practices. Mrinal addresses these critical challenges head-on.

Delayed Disease Identification

A significant delay in disease identification in the Indian aquaculture market results in substantial financial losses, reaching hundreds of millions of dollars annually, along with severe impacts on production volume and employment.

Fixed Cameras and Probes

Stationary cameras provide limited visual coverage of specific zones. Water quality probes offer essential data (pH, O2, Temp), but do not assess individual fish health, leading to incomplete insights.

Basic Acoustic Feeders

Utilizes simple acoustic signals to estimate general fish feeding activity. Offers very basic operational data but lacks specificity for individual fish health, distress, or behavior.

Unlocking Aquaculture's Future

Astram's Mrinal is not just a monitoring device; it's a revolutionary biomimetic robotic fish designed to seamlessly integrate into any aquatic environment. By harmonizing with nature while employing cutting-edge technology, Mrinal provides unprecedented, proactive insights into aquaculture health and operations.

Inspired by Nature the Biomimetic Design

Mrinal's design mimics the natural movement and appearance of fish, allowing it to seamlessly integrate into aquatic environments without causing stress or disruption to the ecosystem. This non-invasive approach ensures accurate data collection without altering fish behavior.

Real-time Visual Sensing

Equipped with high-resolution cameras, Mrinal captures clear, real-time video footage of fish behavior, feeding patterns, and physical health. This visual data is crucial for early detection of anomalies and precise health assessments.

Advanced Acoustic Monitoring

Integrated hydrophones enable Mrinal to monitor underwater sounds, providing insights into fish vocalizations, feeding sounds, and signs of distress. This acoustic data complements visual observations for a holistic understanding of fish well-being.

Intelligent AI-Powered Analysis

Mrinal leverages advanced AI and machine learning algorithms to process vast amounts of data, identifying subtle changes in behavior, detecting early signs of disease, and optimizing feeding strategies for maximum efficiency and fish health.
